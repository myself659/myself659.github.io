45 English sentence pattern

January 16, 2021

询问#

who can#

who can help me move this box?

who is#

who is that girl in blue?

when can we#

when can we go hiking?

When did you#

when did you come back?

where is#

where is the washroom?

where can I#

where can i find some bottled water?

why do/did#

why did you quit your job?

how did you#

How did you get to know each other?

how about#

how about having a cup of coffee?

how can I#

how can i fix my cellphone?

what is#

what is your favorite color?

what are you doing#

what are you looking for?

what do/did#

what do you like to do on weekends?

what did he say just now?

Can I#

can I take a look at your notebook?

Could you/would you#

Could you tell me what time the store is open?

Are you#

Are you making dinner now?

Do you do#

Do you need any help?

Do you mind if I open the window?

Have you#

Have you seen the message I send you?

Is it …#

Is it close to your house?

陈述，解释，介绍#

I am#

I am good at dancing

I am sure that he is sigle

I am not used to spicy food

I am not a big fan of surfing

I am doing#

I am tring to fix my bicycle.

I am going to Florida next month.

I am in#

I am in the hospital now

I want/need#

I want to turn off the heater.

I will do#

I will let you know.

I have/had#

I have a cute cat.

I did#

I went to the grocery store yesterday.

I used to watch this kind of movies whe i was a kid.

Let me/us#

Let me show you the pictures.

Let us get started.

you seem#

you seem upset.

you should#

you should arrive there by 8 o’clock.

you could have done#

you could have kept that marker.

you’d better#

you’d better get up ealiler tomorrow.

you can#

you can take this but to City Hall.

This is#

This is the best cafe in town.

It’s + adj+ to do#

it’s easy to learn how to ride a bicycle.

It’s going to be#

It’s gonna be rainy tomorrow.

It’s said that…#

It’s said that Yoga is very good for health.

It’s no use+doing#

It’s no use complaining all the time.

There’s no way#

There’s no way he can win the game.

There’s no need#

There’s no need to turn on the light.

There’s something wrong with#

There’s something wrong with my TV.

That’s why#

That’s why she chose Social Study as Her major

祈使语句#

Please#

Please have a seat.

Please make sure that you will be there on time.

Do not + do#

Don’t go out after 10:00 PM

Don’t ever buy any gift for me.

虚拟语气#

If i were you, I would#

If i were you, I would, I would by the shoe.

What would you do if#

What would you do if the A/C did not work.
