What is Prompting?#

Prompting” is how humans can talk to artificial intelligence (AI).

“Artificial Intelligence is your paintbrush, data is your paint, but it’s the prompts that guide your hand to create the masterpiece.”

The better the prompt, the better the output.

“Prompt engineering is a relatively new discipline for developing and optimizing prompts to efficiently use LMs for a wide variety of applications and research topics.”

Why is Prompting Important?#

By providing a specific prompt, it’s possible to guide the model to generate output that is most relevant and coherent in context. 通过提供特定的提示，可以引导模型生成在上下文中最相关和连贯的输出。

Prompting allows users to interpret the generated text in a more meaningful way. 提示允许用户以更有意义的方式解释生成的文本。

Prompting is a powerful technique in generative AI that can improve the quality and diversity of the generated text. 提示是生成式 AI 中的一项强大技术，可以提高生成文本的质量和多样性。

Prompting increases control and interpretability, and reduces potential biases. 提示增加了控制和可解释性，并减少了潜在的偏见。

Different models will respond differently to the same prompting, and understanding the specific model can generate precise results with the right prompting. 不同的模型对相同的提示会有不同的反应，了解特定的模型可以在正确的提示下产生精确的结果。

Generative models may hallucinate knowledge that is not factual or incorrect. Prompting can guide the model in the right direction by prompting it to cite correct sources. 生成模型可能会产生不真实或不正确的幻觉知识。提示可以通过提示模型引用正确的来源来引导模型朝着正确的方向发展。

Prompting allows for experimentation with diverse types of data and different ways of presenting that data to the language model. 提示允许对不同类型的数据和将数据呈现给语言模型的不同方式进行实验。

Prompting enables the determination of what good and bad outcomes should look like by incorporating the goal into the prompt. 通过将目标合并到提示中，提示可以确定什么是好结果，什么是坏结果。

Prompting improves the safety of the model and helps defend against prompt hacking (users sending prompts to produce undesired behaviors from the model). 提示提高了模型的安全性并有助于防御提示黑客攻击（用户发送提示以从模型中产生不良行为）。

Challenges#

Achieving the desired results on the first try. 第一次尝试就达到预期的结果。

Finding an appropriate starting point for a prompt. 为提示找到合适的起点。

Ensuring output has minimal biases. 确保输出偏差最小。

Controlling the level of creativity or novelty of the result. 控制结果的创造性或新颖性水平

Understanding and evaluating the reasoning behind the generated responses. 理解和评估生成的响应背后的原因。

Wrong interpretation of the intended meaning of the prompt. 对提示的预期含义的错误解释

Lack of the right balance between providing enough information in the prompt to guide the model and allowing room for novel or creative responses.

在提示中提供足够的信息来指导模型与为新颖或创造性的响应留出空间之间缺乏适当的平衡。

security vulnerabilities#

Prompt injection, where an attacker can manipulate the prompt to generate malicious or harmful output. 提示注入，攻击者可以在其中操纵提示以生成恶意或有害的输出。

Leak sensitive information through the generated output. 通过生成的输出泄露敏感信息

Jailbreaking the model, where an attacker could gain unauthorized access to the model’s internal states and parameters. 越狱模型，攻击者可以在未经授权的情况下访问模型的内部状态和参数。

Generate fake or misleading information. 生成虚假或误导性信息。

The model’s ability to perpetuate societal biases if not trained on diverse and minimally biased data. 如果不对多样化和最小偏见的数据进行训练，该模型有能力使社会偏见永久化。

Generate realistic and convincing text that can be used for malicious or deceitful purposes. 生成可用于恶意或欺骗目的的逼真且令人信服的文本。

The model may generate responses that violate laws or regulations. 该模型可能会生成违反法律或法规的响应。

Question: John has 2 houses. Each house has 3 bedrooms and there are 2 windows in each bedroom.
Each house has 1 kitchen with 2 windows. Also, each house has 5 windows that are not in the bedrooms or kitchens.
How many windows are there in John's houses?
Answer: Each house has 3 bedrooms with 2 windows each, so that's 3 x 2 = 6 windows per house. \
Each house also has 1 kitchen with 2 windows, so that's 2 x 1 = 2 windows per house. \
Each house has 5 windows that are not in the bedrooms or kitchens, so that's 5 x 1 = 5 windows per house. \
In total, each house has 6 + 2 + 5 = 13 windows. \
Since John has 2 houses, he has a total of 2 x 13 = 26 windows. The answer is 26.
Clues: 1. Find the number of bedroom windows, kitchen windows, and other windows separately \
2. Add them together to find the total number of windows at each house \
3. Find the total number of windows for all the houses.

Question: I had 10 pencils. My friend gave me two more. Then, I lost four pencils. How many pencils do I have left?


To solve this question, let's break it down step by step:

Step 1: Start with the number of pencils you had initially, which is 10.

Step 2: Your friend gave you two more pencils, so you need to add those to the initial number. 10 + 2 = 12.

Step 3: You lost four pencils, so you need to subtract that number from the total you had. 12 - 4 = 8.

Therefore, you have 8 pencils left.

Q: Could you recommend the best outline for creating a pitch deck that can help me secure significant investment?

A:
Certainly! Creating a compelling pitch deck is essential for securing significant investment. While the specific content and structure may vary depending on your business and industry, here's a recommended outline that can guide you in creating an effective pitch deck:

1. Title Slide:
   - Company name/logo
   - Your name and position
   - Contact information

2. Problem Statement:
   - Clearly define the problem or pain point your product/service solves
   - Highlight the market need and size of the opportunity

3. Solution:
   - Explain your product/service and how it addresses the problem
   - Highlight the unique value proposition and competitive advantage
   - Use visuals, prototypes, or demos to illustrate your solution

4. Market Analysis:
   - Present market research and industry trends
   - Showcase the target market segment and its growth potential
   - Highlight your target audience and their needs

5. Business Model:
   - Explain how your company generates revenue
   - Outline pricing strategy, distribution channels, and sales approach
   - Discuss any partnerships or key resources that support your business model

6. Go-to-Market Strategy:
   - Detail your marketing and customer acquisition plan
   - Highlight your sales and distribution strategies
   - Discuss your growth projections and expansion plans

7. Competitive Analysis:
   - Identify your direct and indirect competitors
   - Highlight your competitive advantages and barriers to entry
   - Explain how you differentiate yourself from competitors

8. Product/Service Development:
   - Outline your product roadmap and development milestones
   - Highlight any intellectual property (IP) or patents
   - Showcase any achievements, awards, or partnerships

9. Team:
   - Introduce key members of your team and their expertise
   - Highlight relevant experience and track record
   - Demonstrate the team's ability to execute the business plan

10. Financials:
    - Present key financial data, including revenue, expenses, and profitability
    - Show projected financials, including sales forecasts and growth projections
    - Discuss your funding requirements and the use of investment proceeds

11. Investment Opportunity:
    - Clearly state the amount of investment you are seeking
    - Explain how the investment will be utilized and its expected impact
    - Discuss the potential return on investment (ROI) for investors

12. Appendix:
    - Include any additional information, such as market research data, testimonials, or press coverage
    - Attach supporting documents, like financial statements or product images

Remember, keep your pitch deck concise, visually appealing, and focused on the key points. It should tell a compelling story and engage investors while providing a clear understanding of your business and its potential. Adapt the outline as needed to suit your specific business and ensure you tailor the content to your audience. Good luck with your pitch!
